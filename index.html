AI Awareness Web App

A study-focused web application designed to support your thesis workflow: pre-test survey, web app exposure, and post-test survey.

This app introduces basic AI tools, explains what they can do, checks awareness through a self-test, and gives prompt-writing feedback.

Study Flow

  1. Complete the pre-test survey
  2. Use the AI Awareness Web App for one week
  3. Complete the post-test survey

This version stores data in the browser using local storage. You can later connect it to Firebase or Google Sheets if needed.
